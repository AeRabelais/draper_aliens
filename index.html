Our Signal

Sexual health is just health. It belongs alongside dental checkups, broken ankles, and annual physicals — not whispered about in code. SAFE SPACE exists to make that truth feel ordinary, especially for queer folks who've been told otherwise.

💗

Care, not shame

Why It Matters

LGBTQ+ people face steeper barriers to welcoming, informed healthcare — not because of who they are, but because of how systems have treated them. We want to change the weather.

1 in 4

LGBTQ+ adults in the U.S. report avoiding healthcare due to anticipated discrimination.*

99%

Effectiveness of PrEP at preventing HIV from sex when taken as prescribed.

U = U

Meet the Crew

We're a temporary-but-mighty collective that came together around a shared idea: that honest, joyful conversations about sexual health should be the default — especially for queer communities.

No slick HQ, no permanent budget — just a bunch of people in pink shirts who think health deserves better marketing. Every dollar donated goes back into awareness materials, outreach events, and pointing folks toward the real clinics and helplines that already do this work so well.

With love, from somewhere in the pink nebula. 👽💗

Real Help, Real People

Our aliens are friendly, but they're not clinicians. If you need support right now, these humans will take great care of you:

The Trevor Project

24/7 crisis support for LGBTQ+ young people. Call, text, or chat.

Planned Parenthood

Affordable sexual and reproductive healthcare, including STI testing and PrEP.

CDC — PrEP Basics

Official, plain-language info on PrEP and HIV prevention options.

GLMA Provider Directory

Find LGBTQ+-friendly healthcare providers near you.

LGBT National Hotline

Free, confidential peer support — no crisis required to call.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

24/7 mental health support. Dial 988 in the U.S.
